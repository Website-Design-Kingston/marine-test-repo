About This Activity

There are hundreds of shipwrecks in Eastern Lake Ontario and the St. Lawrence. The George A. Marsh is one of the most beautifully preserved wrecks in eastern Lake Ontario and a favorite of recreational divers. Not only is it well preserved as a ship, but divers have been very careful not to remove dozens of artifacts that cover the wreck site.

To visit the wreck, you will be diving 100 feet down to the bottom of Lake Ontario where it is quite cold. You will need a good dry-suit as well as all your regular SCUBA gear. Youâre about to join many other lucky marine explorers who have dived the wreck of the George A. Marsh!
