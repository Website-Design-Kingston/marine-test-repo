Welcome to Shipwrecks

Welcome to the Great Lakes Shipwrecks Virtual Exhibit.

There are hundreds of shipwrecks at the bottom of the Great Lakes and each one is a unique window into Canada's marine history.

But unless you're a trained diver, these incredible artefacts remain hidden beneath the waves!

This is your chance to explore some of these shipwrecks and learn about marine archaeology.
