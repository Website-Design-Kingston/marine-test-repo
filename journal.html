The George A. Marsh was a three masted cargo schooner launched in 1882, used to carry lumber, and then coal. The ship was lost on August 8, 1917 when it sank during a summer gale.

The Marsh today is a fully intact wreck which is a tribute to what can be accomplished if divers respect our marine heritage.
